The Story of Oedipus

The story of Oedipus is a tragic tale from Greek mythology, most famously told in Sophocles' play Oedipus Rex.

Oedipus, the king of Thebes, is prophesied at birth to kill his father and marry his mother. But will his fate become reality?





Characters

Name Description
Oedipus Protagonist and king of Thebes, doomed by fate to kill his father and marry his mother.
Jocasta Oedipus' wife and queen of Thebes.
Laius The former king of Thebes and Oedipus' biological father.
Creon Jocasta's brother and Oedipus' trusted advisor.
Tiresias A blind prophet with the gift of foresight.
The Chorus A group of Theban elders who represent the voice of the people.

Key Themes

  • Fate vs. Free Will
  • Knowledge and Ignorance
  • Guilt and Innocence
  • Blindness and Sight
